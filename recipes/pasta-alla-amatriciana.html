Pasta all'Amatriciana

Description

Pasta all'Amatriciana is a very tasty dish of the Italian tradition that can be prepared very easily.

It's perfect as the main course of a dinner among friends, especially when matched with a good bottle of red wine.

Ingredients

  • 320g of spaghetti
  • 150g of guanciale
  • 400g of tomatoes
  • 75g of pecorino
  • 1 chili pepper
  • salt

Steps

  1. Put some water in a pot, and begin to warm it over a stove.
  2. Cut the guanciale into small stripes. Brown the stripes into a pan.
  3. Put the chili pepper and the tomatoes inside the pan with the browned guanciale.
  4. When the water into the pot starts boiling, put some salt and the spaghetti in it.
  5. Once the spaghetti are cooked, drain them and put them into the guanciale pan.
  6. Grate the pecorino over the spaghetti and mix them together.
  7. The spaghetti all'Amatriciana are ready!
