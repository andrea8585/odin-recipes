Pasta cacio e pepe

Description

Pasta cacio e pepe is an easy and spicy dish of the Italian tradition.

It's perfect as the main course of a lunch or dinner among friends.

Ingredients

  • 200g of spaghetti
  • 50g of pecorino
  • black peppercorns
  • salt

Steps

  1. Put some water in a pot, and begin to warm it over a stove.
  2. Put the peppercorns in a bowl and crush them with a meat pestle
  3. When the water into the pot starts boiling, put some salt and the spaghetti in it.
  4. Put the crushed peppercorns in a pan and brown them. Then add two spoon of cooking water
  5. Put the grated pecorino in abowl with two spoon of cooking water. Stir the mixture with a spoon till it become a cream
  6. Once the spaghetti are cooked, drain them in the pepper pan. Add also the pecorino cream and mix all together with a spoon
  7. The spaghetti cacio e pepe are ready!
