Pasta alla carbonara

Description

Pasta alla carbonara is a very easy and tasty dish of the Italian tradition.

It's perfect as the main course of a lunch, especially when there is not a lot of time for cooking.

Ingredients

  • 320g of spaghetti
  • 6 yolks
  • 150g of guanciale
  • 50g of pecorino
  • black pepper
  • salt

Steps

  1. Put some water in a pot, and begin to warm it over a stove.
  2. Cut the guanciale into small stripes. Brown the stripes into a pan.
  3. Put the yolks, the grated pecorino and some black pepper into a bowl. Mix everything with a spoon till it becomes a smooth cream.
  4. When the water into the pot starts boiling, put some salt and the spaghetti in it.
  5. Once the spaghetti are cooked, drain them and put them into the guanciale pan.
  6. Pour the yolk cream over the spaghetti and mix them together.
  7. The spaghetti alla carbonara are ready!
